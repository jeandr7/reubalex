About

I am a professional and friendly photographer based in Bedfordshire, I have a passion for photographing people. I do headshots, portraits, weddings and events but also other more creative projects. I travel to locations around Bedfordshire and London for events, family portraits, etc. But I can also provide studio based work for more traditional portraits and headshots.


Moments are fleeting but photographs are eternal. I have been passionate about photography my whole life. I started on an old 35mm film camera and have slowly upgraded to state of the art equipment to help me capture every special moment. I am always friendly and accommodating to all my clients and aim to provide a comfortable and professional experience.

FAQ


How would you describe your photography style?

For events and weddings, I do “documentary style” or “candid” photography. This means that I get the basic checklist of shots done that I need, and I spend most of the time capturing natural moments and staying out of the way.

Do you take traditional posed shots?

I do! I can offer studio work in Bedford but I also have portable studio lights for events. What is your pricing? My pricing is affordable and honest and I work with clients to create a package that fits their time and budget.

How many photos will I get

I cannot give an exact number because every situation is different. I also spend a lot of time processing the images to make sure every single one is phenomenal. A good rule of thumb is to expect about 30 images for every hour of shooting

What happens if it rains?

We get wet! All my gear is weather sealed and rainy weather can make for some beautiful photographs.

Do you offer a videography service?

I do only photography, but I work with other professionals and can subcontract a videography service if needed.
